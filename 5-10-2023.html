Theology Made Accessible: A Review of J.I. Packer's 'Knowing God'

As reality sets in and life begins to get hectic, I need to take a minute and apologize for the lack of two blogs to kick off your week. In lieu of MOnday’s absent book review, we’re just going to pick up where we would have left off. As a lover of theology, I’m always on the lookout for books that can offer profound spiritual insights and deepen our understanding of God. One such book that has stood the test of time and continues to impact the lives of countless believers is "Knowing God" by J.I. Packer. Having read this book multiple times, I am convinced that it is a must-read for any Christian seeking to grow in their faith and enrich their relationship with God. In this book, I will provide a summary of "Knowing God," highlight its key themes, and explain why it is an essential read for all Christians. Summary of "Knowing God"

First published in 1973, "Knowing God" has since become a classic in Christian literature. J.I. Packer, a prominent British-born Canadian theologian, authored this transformative work to help believers develop a more intimate understanding of God and experience a deeper relationship with Him. The book is divided into three sections, with each section building upon the previous one to form a comprehensive understanding of God's character and His relationship with humanity.

Section One: Know the Lord

The first section of "Knowing God" is an invitation to know God personally, recognizing the importance of developing an intimate relationship with Him. Packer emphasizes that knowing God is not merely about acquiring intellectual knowledge, but rather about experiencing His presence and developing a deep-rooted love for Him. Packer provides an overview of God's attributes, such as His omnipotence, omniscience, and omnipresence, and explains how these qualities reveal His unchanging nature and faithfulness.

Section Two: Behold Your God

In the second section, Packer delves deeper into the character of God, examining His various attributes in greater detail. He discusses God's wisdom, goodness, and holiness, as well as His wrath, justice, and love. Packer highlights the importance of understanding these attributes as they offer insight into God's nature and His dealings with humanity. By recognizing God's perfect balance of justice and mercy, believers can appreciate the magnitude of His grace and forgiveness.

Section Three: If God Be For Us

The final section of "Knowing God" explores the implications of knowing God for the life of a believer. Packer discusses the impact of God's grace on our lives, emphasizing the transformative power of the gospel. He also goes into the role of the Holy Spirit in the believer's life, the concept of adoption into God's family, and the assurance of salvation. This section serves as a practical guide for living out the Christian faith and experiencing the fullness of God's blessings.

Key Themes in "Knowing God"
  • The Importance of Knowing God: Packer emphasizes that the ultimate purpose of human life is to know God and to glorify Him. This theme resonates throughout the book, as he consistently highlights the significance of developing an intimate relationship with God.
  • The Character of God: "Knowing God" provides a comprehensive exploration of God's attributes, allowing readers to gain a deeper understanding of His character. By examining these attributes, Packer encourages believers to appreciate the greatness of God and develop a more profound love for Him.
  • The Transformative Power of the Gospel: Packer underscores the power of the gospel to transform lives and bring believers into a close relationship with God. He emphasizes that God's grace, manifested through the work of Jesus Christ, is sufficient to save and transform even the most undeserving sinners.
  • The Role of the Holy Spirit: The book highlights the importance of the Holy Spirit in the life of a believer, emphasizing the need for spiritual growth and sanctification. Packer explains that the Holy Spirit serves as our guide, comforter, and teacher, enabling us to develop a deeper relationship with God and empowering us to live in obedience to His will.
  • Assurance of Salvation: Packer addresses the assurance of salvation, explaining that believers can have confidence in their eternal security through faith in Jesus Christ. He emphasizes that this assurance is grounded in the promises of God and not in our feelings or circumstances.
Why "Knowing God" Is a Must-Read for Christians
  • Timeless Wisdom: As a classic in Christian literature, "Knowing God" provides insights that remain relevant and impactful even decades after its initial publication. The book's emphasis on the character of God and the importance of knowing Him personally resonates with believers of all ages and stages of spiritual growth.
  • Spiritual Enrichment: "Knowing God" challenges readers to develop a deeper understanding of God and His ways, resulting in spiritual growth and enrichment. The book encourages believers to move beyond a superficial understanding of God and to seek an intimate relationship with Him.
  • Practical Application: Packer's writing provides practical guidance on how to live out the Christian faith, focusing on the transformative power of the gospel and the role of the Holy Spirit in the believer's life. Readers are encouraged to apply the truths explored in the book to their daily lives, experiencing the fullness of God's blessings.
  • Accessible and Engaging: Despite tackling complex theological concepts, Packer's writing is clear, concise, and engaging. Readers will appreciate his ability to explain deep spiritual truths in a manner that is accessible and relatable, making "Knowing God" an enjoyable read for Christians of all levels of biblical understanding.
  • Encouragement and Hope: Throughout "Knowing God," Packer offers encouragement and hope to believers, reminding them of the steadfast love, grace, and mercy of God. This message serves as a powerful reminder of the sufficiency of Christ's sacrifice and the assurance of our salvation, providing comfort and peace in an often uncertain world.
Conclusion

In conclusion, "Knowing God" by J.I. Packer is a must-read for any Christian seeking to grow in their faith and enrich their relationship with God. The book's timeless wisdom, spiritual enrichment, practical application, accessibility, and message of hope make it an essential addition to every believer's library. As we dive into the depths of "Knowing God," we are reminded of the profound privilege it is to know the Creator of the universe intimately and personally. May this book serve as a catalyst for spiritual growth and inspire a deeper love and appreciation for the God who loves us beyond measure.
